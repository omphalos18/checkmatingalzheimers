Get Involved

Your move matters.

Whether you're a chess player, a student, a caregiver, or someone who simply cares about Alzheimer's — there is a place for you in this initiative.

Three Ways to Help

Pick your move.

Every contribution — big or small — directly advances our mission.

01

Play in a Tournament

Join one of our community chess tournaments across Upstate and Midlands South Carolina. All skill levels are welcome — beginners to advanced.

  • All skill levels welcome
  • Tournaments across multiple SC cities
  • Entry fees go to the Alzheimer's Association
  • Part of the #TheLongestDay campaign
  • "Do What You Love to End Alz" events
Sign Up for Alerts
02

Volunteer With Us

Help teach chess to seniors, organize events, or spread awareness at your school. Your time directly shapes how far we reach.

  • Teach chess at retirement communities
  • Help organize local tournaments
  • Spread awareness at your school
  • Mentor new chess players
  • No chess experience required to help
Volunteer Now
03

Donate & Support

Can't make it to a tournament? Donate directly to the Alzheimer's Association through our fundraising campaign — every dollar funds real research.

  • Support the Alzheimer's Association
  • Fund "Do What You Love to End Alz"
  • Support Clemson research recruitment
  • Help fund senior chess workshops
  • 100% of donations go to the cause
